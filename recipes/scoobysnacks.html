scooby snacks from Scooby Dooby Doo



Description

The most famous snack from the detective show scooby doo. Where Shaggy and his pet and best friend Scooby take down the bad guys while chomping down on scooby snacks make the addition to afternoon tea!

Ingredients

  • 16 small (1-inch) chewy chocolate candies, unwrapped
  • 5 paper lollipop sticks
  • 1 box Betty Crocker™ Fruit Roll-Ups™ Blastin' Berry Hot Colors™ chewy fruit flavored snacks
  • 5 mini candy-coated chocolate pieces
  • 1 container Betty Crocker™ Whipped fluffy white frosting
  • 1 tube (0.68 oz) Betty Crocker™ black decorating gel
  • Block of white plastic foam

Steps

  1. Microwave 2 chewy chocolate candies on High about 10 seconds, just until moldable. Shape each into rectangle about 2 1/4 inches long and 1 inch wide. Press candies together onto stick. Repeat to make 5 pops.

  2. Microwave each additional chocolate candy 5 to 8 seconds, just until moldable. Cut off about 2 (1/8 inch) pieces to shape into dog ears. Attach to dog between 2 candies at top using small amount of water and pressing candies together. Cut off 2 additional very small pieces and roll and flatten to form into dog cheeks, and attach with water. Attach mini chocolate candy-coated candy with small amount of frosting for nose.

  3. Cut out ears and tongue from red portion of Sizzling Red/Yellow Fruit Roll-Ups snacks; attach using small amount of water. Cut out blue collar from Blazin' Blue/Green Fruit Roll-Ups snacks about 2 inches long and 1/8 inch wide; wrap around neck, and attach using water. Cut out doubled up 3/8-inch yellow square from yellow portion of Fruit Roll-Ups snacks. Cut out smaller blue square; attach to yellow square and collar using small amount of water.

  4. Place about 1/2 cup frosting into sandwich-size resealable plastic bag; cut off tiny corner of bag. Pipe eyes on dog. Add drop of black to make pupils and eyebrows using black gel. Insert into cup or jar with foam base to support pops.
